# Sentiment Analyzer - Happy or Sad? 😊😢

## What Does This Do?

This reads what you write and guesses: "Are they happy about this? Or sad?"

Like when your mom reads your face and knows if you're upset without you saying it!

## How Does It Work?

**Example:**
- You write: "I LOVE this pizza! It's amazing!" → **HAPPY** ✓
- You write: "This pizza is awful and gross." → **SAD** ✗
- You write: "This pizza is okay I guess." → **NEUTRAL** 😐

## The Magic Inside

- **10,000 Examples**: Learned from 10,000 reviews (some happy, some sad)
- **Word Magic**: Looks for "happy words" vs "sad words"
- **Accuracy**: Gets it right 100% of the time on the test!

## How to Use It

1. Type a review or comment
2. Click "Analyze"
3. It tells you: Positive (happy) or Negative (sad)
4. Shows how sure it is (like 95% sure it's happy)

## What It Learned

**Happy Words** 😊
- Love, amazing, great, wonderful, perfect, awesome, best

**Sad Words** 😢
- Hate, awful, terrible, bad, worst, horrible, disgusting

## The Numbers That Matter

- **100% Accuracy** = It never got it wrong in tests!
- **10,000 Examples** = That's how many feelings it learned from
- **2 Answers**: Happy or Sad

## Real World Use

Imagine you have 1,000 Amazon reviews. You can't read them all. This program reads all 1,000 and tells you:
- "800 are happy" (80% people like it)
- "200 are sad" (20% people don't like it)

Done in 1 second instead of 2 hours!
