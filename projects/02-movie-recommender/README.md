# Movie Recommender 🍿

## What Does This Do?

This is like your friend who knows you really well and says "Hey! I think you'll like THIS movie!"

It watches what movies YOU like, then finds other people who like the same stuff, and recommends what THEY liked. Like finding your movie buddy!

## How Does It Work?

**Example:**
1. You loved "Toy Story" and "Monsters Inc"
2. Someone else also loved those two
3. But they ALSO loved "Finding Nemo"
4. So it tells you: "Try Finding Nemo - you might like it!"

## The Magic Inside

- **Movies**: 1,682 different movies
- **Ratings**: 100,000 people rated these movies
- **Smart Trick**: It finds people with similar taste to you
- **Method**: Looks for movies that are "similar" to what you like

## How Similar Things Work

Imagine you like:
- Action movies ✓
- Superhero movies ✓
- Movies with fast cars ✓

So if another movie has all three things, you probably like it too!

## How to Use It

1. Pick a movie you love from the list
2. Click "Get Recommendations"
3. See 5 movies similar to your choice
4. Each one shows how similar it is (0.5 means 50% similar)

## The Numbers That Matter

- **1,682 Movies** = That's how many we can recommend
- **100,000 Ratings** = People told us what they liked
- **Top 5 Results** = You get the 5 best matches

## What Makes It Smart

It's not magic - it's math. It finds patterns:
- People who like A also like B
- People who like B also like C
- So people who like A probably like C too!

---

**Simple Truth**: It's like asking 100,000 people "What should I watch?" and picking the best answer!
